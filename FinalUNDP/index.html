Inequality and Education

Inequality is measured by Gini coefficient. The higher the Gini, the more unequal a country is. The deficiency in education enrollment has become one of the major factors that contribute to the inequalities in Sub-Saharan African countries. This visualization aims to investigate the relationships between inequality and enrollment rates in primary, secondary and tertiary education, with an additional scope of looking at the differences in enrollment from both female and male population.

Gross Enrollment Rate

Gross enrollment ratio is the ratio of total enrollment, regardless of age, to the population of the age group that officially corresponds to the level of education shown.
